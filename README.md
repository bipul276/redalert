# Product Recall Alert Hub (RedAlert)

## Product Vision
A platform that aggregates recall alerts from government and news sources to notify users of safety risks relevant to them.

## Scope (MVP)
- **Regions**: USA, India.
- **Sources**: CPSC, FDA, NHTSA, Tier-1 News (India).
- **Alerts**: Email only.
- **Platform**: Web (Next.js) + PWA.

## Disclaimer
> **IMPORTANT**: This platform aggregates publicly available information. It is not an official regulatory body. Always follow official manufacturer or government instructions. We do not predict defects or provide legal/medical advice.

## Technology Stack
- **Backend**: Python (FastAPI) + spaCy (NLP).
- **Frontend**: Next.js (TypeScript) + Tailwind CSS.
- **Database**: PostgreSQL.
